UNIFORM GUIDELINES
As a team member, you represent Wood Ranch. Your appearance should impress our 3 Circles. If a manager determines that your attire or appearance is inappropriate or extreme, you may be sent home to change.
Shirt:   Men: Long –sleeved, fitted, black oxford style button down shirt. No undershirt can be seen.
Women: Conservative sweater or top, grey, black or white in color only. Uncovered shoulders are not acceptable, sleeveless shirts can be worn under a sweater or blazer only. No hoodies.
Bottoms:   Men: Black dress pant. No Dickies style, denim, corduroy, leggings or workout pants.
    Women: Black, white or grey in color only. Dress or skirt (must be no shorter than one inch above the knee in length) or dress pant (full length or capri, no Dickies style, denim, corduroy, leggings or workout pants).
- FOOD RUNNER/BUSSER/SERVER/BARTENDER -
Shirt:   Black oxford style, button down shirt ¾ length sleeve or longer.
Women: have the option of wearing a black, not-faded v-neck knit shirt, ¾ length sleeve or longer.
Pants:   Dark blue (Navy) jean, straight leg.
Apron:   Food Runner, Busser, Server: Chalk-striped Bistro apron (provided). Bar: (provided) WR Logo’d bar apron.
  Women: Black, white or grey in color only. Dress or skirt (must be no shorter than one inch above the knee in length) or dress pant (full length or capri, no Dickies style, denim, corduroy, leggings or workout pants).
- TAKE OUT -
Shirt:   Packer: Black polo Cashier: Black oxford style, button down shirt ¾ length sleeve or longer. Women: have the option of wearing a black, not-faded v-neck knit shirt, ¾ length sleeve or longer.
Pants:   Dark blue (Navy) jean, straight leg.
Apron:   Chalk-stripe Bistro apron (provided).
Hat: Wood Ranch Hat (provided).
- CATERER -Full-Service. For Delivery uniform - see server.
Shirt:   Black oxford style, button down shirt ¾ length sleeve or longer. On specified events: Black polo.
Tie:   Traditional neck tie with Windsor or half Windsor knot. Tie must be tasteful and not tucked into shirt.
Pants: Black dress pant.
Hair:   Server: (provided) Chalk-stripe Bistro apron.
- CULINARY -Full-Service. For Delivery uniform - see server.
Shirt:   Solid black crew neck t-shirt or polo. Only a WR logo is acceptable.
pants:   Traditional neck tie with Windsor or half Windsor knot. Tie must be tasteful and not tucked into shirt.
Pants:   Black dress pant.
Hair:   Server: (provided) Chalk-stripe Bistro apron.
- ALL SERVICE POSITIONS -
Jewelry:   Earrings must be a matching pair and worn in separate ears, two maximum per ear. No longer than one inch. Sizings are not acceptable. One bracelet, one watch, one necklace and one ring is acceptable.
Piercings: One nose piercing is acceptable only if the stud is no larger in size than a 20 gauge. No other visible body piercings are acceptable,
Hair:   Our goal is to present a well-groomed and natural appearance. We expect your hair to be clean, and you should avoid extreme/trendy hair styles or colors. If your hair is longer than shoulder length, braid it, put it in a ponytail, or tightly secure it without any loose strands. Hair accessories should be minimal and consistent with your hair color. If you’re unsure whether your appearance meets our guidelines, please consult with your General Manager. Hosts can wear their hair down, if it’s styled.
- CULINARY - Full-Service. For Delivery uniform - see server.
Shirt:   Solid black crew neck t-shirt or polo. Only a WR logo is acceptable.
Pants::   Black Cotton/Polyester Blend pant; wrinkle-free, non-faded and classic dress pant style. Proper fit, worn at waist, no baggy or tight fit pants. No extra large pockets, extra zippers, etc.
Hat & Apron:   Wood Ranch hat & black apron (provided).
Jewelry & Piercings:   No jewelry (watches, wristband, bracelets, earrings, nose or visible body piercings). Wedding bands are acceptable. A necklace is acceptable – as long as it is small and must be kept underneath shirt to prevent falling.
Fingernails:   No nail polish or artificial nails allowed.
Hair:   Our goal is to present a well-groomed and natural appearance. We expect your hair to be clean, and you should avoid extreme/trendy hair styles or colors. Hair must be pulled back at all times.
- ALL POSITIONS - 
Shirts: Must be fitted, wrinkle free, clean, not-faded, worn tucked in and remain tucked in while performing work duties. No logos. First or second button on the shirt can be unbuttoned. If undershirt is worn, it must be a matching color.
Pants:   Proper fit, worn at waist, no baggy or tight fit pants.
Apron:   (provided) Clean and crisp, worn at waist. If a belt is worn, apron covers belt, tie the apron in front.
Shoes:   Solid black shoe, dress shoe. Closed toe and closed heel. Shoe must be designated slip-resistant.
Socks:   Solid black
Make-up:   (women only): If worn, must be conservative and natural-looking.      
Beards:   If worn, beard must be well-groomed, trimmed and conservatively presentable.
Belt:   (optional): Solid black with no designs or accessories.
Tattoos:   Any visible tattoo(s) that may be considered offensive by your 3 Circles must be covered. We reserve the right to ask you to cover any tattoo deemed to be inappropriate.
