At-Will Employment Statement

This acknowledges that I understand that nothing in the Employee Handbook creates or is intended to create a promise or representation of continues employment and that my employment, position, and compensation at Wood Ranch BBQ & Grill are “at-will” and may be changed or terminated at the will of the Company.

I further understand that my status as an “at-will” employee may not be changed except in writing by the President of the Company. My signature below certifies that I understand the foregoing agreement that at-will status is the sole and entire agreement between the Company and myself concerning the duration of my employment and the circumstances under which my employment may be terminated. It supersedes any and all prior agreements, understandings and representations (whether written or oral) concerning my employment with the Company.

 

My signature below attests to the fact that I have read, understand, and agree to be legally bound to all of the above terms. I further acknowledge that this provision has been explained to me in a language or manner that I understand.
